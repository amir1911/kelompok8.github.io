Why Skincare is
Important For You

Kulit adalah lapisan pelindung pertama tubuh dari polusi, sinar UV, dan kotoran. Perawatan kulit yang tepat membantu menjaga kelembapan, mencegah penuaan dini, dan mengurangi risiko masalah kulit seperti jerawat atau iritasi.

Kulit yang sehat dan terawat membuat Anda merasa lebih nyaman dan percaya diri. Dengan skincare yang tepat, kulit terlihat lebih bersih, segar, dan bercahaya— membantu Anda tampil maksimal setiap hari.

Know How To Glow Based From
Your Skin Type

Normal Skin

Normal skin is well-balanced, neither too oily nor too dry, with small, barely visible pores, a smooth texture, and minimal imperfections.

Check Your Skincare Stages

Oily Skin

Oily skin produces excess sebum, giving it a shiny appearance and making it prone to acne, blackheads, and enlarged pores. It requires oil-controlling products to maintain a healthy look.

Check Your Skincare Stages

Sensitive

For those with sensitive skin, irritation is a common issue, leading to redness, itching, and discomfort when exposed to harsh skincare products or environmental factors. This skin type benefits from gentle, fragrance-free, and hypoallergenic formulas.

Check Your Skincare Stages

Combination Skin

Combination skin is a mix of different skin types, typically having an oily T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin) while the cheeks remain dry, requiring a balanced skincare approach.
