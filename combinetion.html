Combination Skin

Combination skin is characterized by a mix of different skin types on different areas of the face. Typically, the T-zone (forehead, nose, and chin) tends to be oilier, while the cheeks and other areas can be normal or dry. This dual nature requires a balanced skincare approach that addresses both oiliness and dryness.
