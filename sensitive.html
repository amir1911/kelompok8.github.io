Sensitive Skin

Normal skin is characterized by a well-balanced moisture and oil level, ensuring that it is neither excessively oily nor overly dry. It has a smooth, even texture with a healthy, natural glow. The pores are small and barely visible, contributing to a refined and uniform appearance. This skin type is typically free from frequent breakouts, flakiness, or excessive shine. It maintains good elasticity and suppleness, with minimal sensitivity or redness. While occasional blemishes or dryness may occur due to environmental factors, normal skin generally remains clear, resilient, and easy to maintain with a consistent skincare routine.
